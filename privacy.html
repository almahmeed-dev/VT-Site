Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 12, 2024

Summary: Vitals Translator processes all your health data entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal or health information. Your privacy is absolute.

1. Information We Collect

We collect no personal information.

Vitals Translator is designed with privacy as a core principle. The app:

  • Does not require account creation
  • Does not collect your name, email, or any identifying information
  • Does not use analytics or tracking services
  • Does not contain advertising

2. Health Data

Vitals Translator reads the following data from Apple Health with your explicit permission:

  • Heart Rate Variability (HRV)
  • Resting Heart Rate
  • Sleep Analysis
  • Active Energy Burned
  • Step Count

This health data is processed entirely on your device. It is never transmitted to our servers or any third party. We have no ability to access your health information.

3. Data Storage

All data generated by the app (your vitals scores, insights, and reports) is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure storage mechanisms. If you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed.

4. Third-Party Services

Vitals Translator does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The only external service used is Apple's HealthKit, which operates under Apple's privacy policies.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share any data because we do not have access to any data. Your information stays on your device.

You may choose to share your weekly reports using the built-in share feature. This is entirely voluntary and controlled by you.

6. Children's Privacy

Vitals Translator is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@vitalstranslator.app
